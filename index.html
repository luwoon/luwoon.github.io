About me

Currently a Statistics Senior Executive in Central Narcotics Bureau, I work extensively with data to derive insights on policy formulation and operational efficiency.

Data analytics

I utilise IBM Cognos Analytics and Excel to clean and visualise data. I automate data processing tasks by creating SQL scripts and management dashboards, cutting manual workload by about 75%.

Communicating insights

I analyse drug trends and present data-driven recommendations to senior management and regional stakeholders quarterly. I also provide expert guidance and training on data analysis tools and techniques to staff.

Other skills

I believe in continuous upskilling. In my free time, I learn and create projects using different analytics tools (Tableau, Power BI) and programming languages (R, Python, etc.)

I work closely with other staff in the organization, senior management, and local and stakeholders to understand their data requirements and present data findings to them. I believe my proven skills in working with large datasets and deriving meaningful data insights are transferable to other industries, which I am eager to explore.

A musician

I am a pianist and a violist. Currently, I am pursuing the Licentiate of Trinity College London (LTCL) diploma qualification for piano, on top of my existing ABRSM Grade 8 qualifications in the piano, viola, and music theory. In my free time, I also perform in various orchestras in Singapore as a member and a guest player on the viola.

Previously, I taught music at Cristofori Music Pte Ltd, and was a freelance piano accompanist.
